Who made Ice cream?

There is no ONE person who made ice cream. Many different people made it their own way.

The History

Where When Description
China Around 200 BCE Milk and rice frozen in snow.
Persia Around 500 BCE Fruit juices mixed in snow.
Rome 1st century AD Fruit and honey mixed in snow.
Italy 1565 This version used milk, honey, egg yolk, and wine.
France 1553 but the first recipies came in 1686. This version used cream, milk, butter, and eggs; later versions added sugar and vanilla.
United States 1744 This version used cream, sugar, and strawberries.
